Welcome to the SeaOdyssey!

Your task is to find all 10 ships within 66 moves.

The ships you are about to sink are:
1) one ship the length of 4
2) two ships the length of 3
3) three ships the length of 2
4) four ships the length of 1

Blue square means you have drowned your bullet.
Brown square means you have reached the ship.

You can upload new game by clicking the New Game button.

Good luck!
